Committees & Agendas

Explore our committees and their agendas, designed to address global challenges and foster leadership.

UNSC

UNSC (United Nations Security Council)

Reassessing the Legality and Security Implications of Extraterritorial Counter-terrorism Operations Conducted Without Host State Consent.

UNHRC

UNHRC (United Nations Human Rights Council)

Addressing Human Rights' Violations due to Police / State Intervention during Protests & Peaceful Assemblies

UNCSW

UNCSW (United Nations Commission on the Status of Women)

Strengthening Global Frameworks to Prevent Sexual Exploitation and Trafficking of Women and Girls by Powerful Networks: Enhancing Accountability, Survivor-Centric Justice, and Institutional Transparency.

LOKSABHA

National Security and Civil Liberties: Re-examination of Sedition, UAPA, and Preventive Detention Laws.
